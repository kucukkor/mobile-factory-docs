# Privacy Policy for KVADRA

**Last Updated:** May 7, 2026

At "Ismail Küçükkör" (Istanbul), we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data in "KVADRA".

## 1. Data We Do NOT Collect
We do not have a custom backend. We do not collect or store personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number on our own servers.

## 2. Third-Party Services
We use third-party SDKs to provide essential game features. These services may collect data according to their own policies:
- **Google Play Game Services / Apple Game Center:** For leaderboards, achievements, and cloud saves.
- **Google AdMob:** For displaying advertisements. They may use device identifiers to serve personalized ads.
- **Unity/Godot Analytics:** (If enabled) For anonymous gameplay statistics to improve the game.

## 3. Data Deletion
Since we do not store your data on our servers, account deletion is handled through the respective platform services (Google Play or Apple ID). You can manage or delete your game data via your device settings or the Game Services dashboard.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions, please contact us at: info@esimostudios.com
