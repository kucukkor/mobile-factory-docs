# Privacy Policy for SVARM

**Last Updated:** July 9, 2026

This policy explains how "SVARM — Co-op Survival" handles your data. SVARM is an
online co-op game, so it has its own game server. This document describes what
that server stores and which third parties are involved.

## 1. Data SVARM Collects

SVARM keeps your progress on its own server so it follows your account across
devices. We store the minimum needed to run the game:

- **Account**: an internal account identifier, a public player code, and an
  auto-generated nickname. A guest account is **anonymous** — created on the
  device with no personal information. If you choose to sign in with Apple or
  Google to protect your progress, we store only the provider's **subject
  identifier** (an opaque id). **We do not store your name, email address, phone
  number, precise location, or contacts.**
- **Game progression**: in-game gold, weapon unlocks, upgrade levels, and match
  results.
- **Purchase records**: which in-app items you have bought, so the server can
  grant the corresponding currency or unlocks and restore them later.

All data is transmitted over **encrypted connections** (HTTPS / secure
WebSocket). We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising
profiling on our server.

### Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account at any time from **Settings → Account →
Delete account** inside the game. This is irreversible: your progress and in-game
currency are erased. Purchase records required for legal/accounting purposes may
be retained in anonymized form.

## 2. Third-Party Services

### 2.1 Sign in with Apple / Google Sign-In (optional)

Only used if you choose to link your account for cross-device recovery. Sign-in
is handled by your device; SVARM receives a verified identifier and stores only
that opaque id — not your name or email.

- Apple: <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>
- Google: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>

### 2.2 RevenueCat (in-app purchase processing)

Processes and restores in-app purchases. Purchases are associated with your
SVARM account so currency and unlocks can be granted and restored.

- <https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/>

### 2.3 Google AdMob (rewarded + interstitial ads)

SVARM shows an optional rewarded ad (to revive) and interstitial ads between
runs. AdMob uses your **Advertising ID** to serve ads. On iOS, SVARM respects
App Tracking Transparency — if you decline, AdMob serves non-personalized ads.

- <https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads>

### 2.4 App Stores (Apple App Store / Google Play)

Payments are processed by the store. We never see your payment method.

## 3. Children

SVARM is intended for adults (18+) due to its combat theme and is not directed
to children.

## 4. Changes

We may update this policy; the "Last Updated" date reflects the latest version.

## 5. Contact

Questions about your data can be sent through the in-game contact option.
