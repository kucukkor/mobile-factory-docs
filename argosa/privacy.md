# Privacy Policy for ARGOSA

**Last Updated:** July 5, 2026

At "Esimo Studios" (Istanbul), we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data in "Argosa: Sea Trade Empire" ("ARGOSA").

## 1. Data We Do NOT Collect Directly

ARGOSA does not have its own backend. We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location to our own servers.

## 2. Third-Party Services Used by ARGOSA

ARGOSA uses the following third-party services to deliver core features. Each is described with what it does and what data it processes.

### 2.1 Apple Game Center / Google Play Games Services

Used for leaderboards and achievements only. Sign-in is handled by your device's operating system (Apple ID / Google Account) — ARGOSA does not store your platform handle or Game Center / Play Games Services credentials.

- Apple's privacy policy: <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>
- Google's privacy policy: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>

### 2.2 RevenueCat (in-app purchase processing)

Used to deliver and restore in-app purchases (the "No More Dispatches" ad-removal non-consumable, gold pack consumables, and the Merchant's Pack bundle). RevenueCat operates in **anonymous mode** for ARGOSA — we do not send your name or contact details to it. RevenueCat associates your purchases with the App Store / Google Play receipt that Apple or Google generates on your behalf.

- RevenueCat's privacy policy: <https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/>

### 2.3 Google AdMob (interstitial + rewarded ads)

ARGOSA shows interstitial ads between voyages and optional rewarded ads (double sale, free repair, harbormaster's price tip). AdMob uses your **Advertising ID** (IDFA / GAID) to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. On iOS, ARGOSA requests App Tracking Transparency permission solely for this ad personalization — if you decline, AdMob serves non-personalized ads. Purchasing "No More Dispatches" disables interstitial ads entirely.

- Google AdMob privacy: <https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads>

### 2.4 App Stores (Apple App Store / Google Play Store)

When you purchase an item through ARGOSA, the App Store or Google Play processes the transaction. We never see your payment method.

## 3. Services We Do NOT Use

To be explicit, ARGOSA v1.0 does **NOT** integrate:

- Facebook Login, Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In (other than the platform Game Center / Play Games sign-in described above)
- Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Crashlytics, Sentry, or any other crash-reporting / analytics SDK beyond what AdMob includes
- iCloud or Google Drive cloud-save sync for game progress

If we add any of these to a future version, this policy will be updated at least 7 days before the update ships.

## 4. Local Data

ARGOSA stores game progress (your voyage — gold, debt, ship, cargo, captain skills, commissions, world day — plus settings and locale preference) **locally on your device** in the app's sandboxed user-data folder. This data never leaves your device, except for the purchase receipts described in §2.2 and ad-targeting data described in §2.3.

## 5. Children's Privacy

ARGOSA is suitable for ages 7+. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover that your child has provided us with information you did not authorize, contact us at the email below and we will help.

## 6. Data Deletion

Since ARGOSA does not store data on our own servers, the data we hold about you consists of:

- Locally stored game progress on your device — deleting the app removes this data.
- Purchase records held by the App Store / Google Play / RevenueCat — these are governed by their respective providers' policies. You can revoke RevenueCat's record by contacting us (§7) and we will request deletion via RevenueCat's data subject request process.
- Leaderboard / achievement records held by Apple Game Center / Google Play Games Services — managed via your Apple ID / Google Account settings.
- AdMob ad-targeting data — managed via Google's Ads Settings on Android and Apple's Tracking settings on iOS.
